# ZNF527

## Overview
ZNF527 is a human gene that encodes the zinc finger protein 527, a member of the zinc finger protein family characterized by the presence of zinc finger motifs. These motifs are typically involved in binding to DNA, RNA, or other proteins, suggesting a role in transcriptional regulation. Zinc finger proteins are often implicated in various cellular processes, including development, differentiation, and apoptosis.
