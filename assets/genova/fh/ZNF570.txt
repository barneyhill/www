These proteins are characterized by the presence of zinc finger motifs, which are small protein structural motifs that can coordinate one or more zinc ions to help stabilize their folds. Zinc finger protein 570 is involved in various cellular processes, including transcriptional regulation, by binding to DNA and influencing the expression of other genes.
