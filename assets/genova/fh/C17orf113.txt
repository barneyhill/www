# C17orf113

## Overview
C17orf113 is a gene located on chromosome 17 that encodes the protein known as chromosome 17 open reading frame 113. This protein is categorized as an uncharacterized protein, meaning its specific biological functions and roles within cellular processes are not yet fully understood. Despite the limited information on its precise function, studies have suggested potential involvement in various cellular pathways, although further research is necessary to elucidate its exact mechanisms and interactions. The gene and its encoded protein are subjects of ongoing research due to their potential implications in human health and disease (Qiu2020aarFDC).
