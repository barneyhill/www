# RUSF1

## Overview
RUSF1 is a gene that encodes the protein RUS family member 1, which is involved in various cellular processes. The RUSF1 protein is categorized as a regulatory protein, playing a crucial role in modulating cellular pathways and maintaining cellular homeostasis. It is implicated in the regulation of gene expression and signal transduction, contributing to the intricate network of cellular communication. The protein's function is essential for normal cellular operations, and its dysregulation has been associated with several pathological conditions, highlighting its clinical significance (Qiu2020aarFDC).
