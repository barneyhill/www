# C10orf88

## Overview
C10orf88 is a gene located on chromosome 10 that encodes the protein known as chromosome 10 open reading frame 88. This protein is categorized as a non-kinase, non-receptor protein, and its specific biological functions are not yet fully elucidated. However, it is implicated in various cellular processes and may play a role in the regulation of gene expression and cellular signaling pathways. The protein's interactions and potential involvement in disease pathways are subjects of ongoing research, highlighting its significance in understanding complex biological systems and potential clinical implications (Qiu2020aarFDC).
